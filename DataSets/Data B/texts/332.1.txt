It is of upmost importance to take the following preventive measures: Avoid contact with (deceased) Ebola patients and / or their body fluids.Avoid burial rites involving contact with the deceased Ebola patient. Avoid contact with materials of the patient potentially infected (eg. Syringes, clothing, linens, eating utensils). Avoid contact with live or dead wild animals (monkeys, bats, bongos). Do not eat raw meat or bush meat. Avoid unprotected sexual intercourse with a cured Ebola patient for seven weeks after healing.
