Symptoms of epidemic typhus may include:Chills,Confusion,Cough,Delirium,High fever (104 degrees Fahrenheit),Joint pain (arthralgia),Lights that appear very bright; light may hurt the eyes,Low blood pressure,Rash that begins on the chest and spreads to the rest of the body (except the palms of the hands and soles of the feet),Severe headache,Severe muscle pain (myalgia),Stupor
