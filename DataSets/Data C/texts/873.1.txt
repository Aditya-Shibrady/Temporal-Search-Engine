Due to Ebola some patients also bleed from the nose and mouth. This is called hemorrhagic syndrome and usually occurs only in the late stages of the disease. Typically, the Ebola virus causes hemorrhagic syndrome in 30 to 50 percent of patients. Other symptoms that occur in advanced stages of EVD include rash and symptoms of impairedkidney and liver function, such as blood in the stoolses, bleeding in the brain can lead to severe depression,seizures and delirium.
