On August 27th, 2014, WHO informed about a Ebola virus outbreak in Democratic Republic of Congo with24 cases and 13 deaths and on September 2nd 2014, a virological analysis confirmed that this is separated and independent event from the current Ebola outbreak in West Africa, having the Zaire virus strain, in alineage most closely related to a virus from the 1995 Ebola outbreak in Kikwit, Democratic Republic of Congo.As of November 9th, this outbreak has a total of 66 cases (38 confirmed and 28 probable) including eight casesamong health-care workers (HCW). In total, 49 deaths have been reported, including eight among HCW.
