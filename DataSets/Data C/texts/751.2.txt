Epidemic typhus (European, Classic, Louse-borne)Epidemic typhus is prevalent worldwide. It is an acute disease passed from human to human by the body louse. Endemic foci of epidemic typhus exist in highland populations in Africa and South America, but tourists are at minimal risk of acquiring lice and disease.The disease is characterized by high fever, intractable headache, and rash.
