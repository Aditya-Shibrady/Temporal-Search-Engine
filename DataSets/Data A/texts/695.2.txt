Cholera germs are found in the feces (poop) of infected people.Cholera is spread when feces (poop) from an infected person gets into the water people drink or the food people eat. Cholera is not likely to spread directly from one person to another.If you have oral rehydration solution (ORS), start taking it now; it can save your life.Go immediately to the nearest health facility. Continue to drink ORS at home and while you travel to get treatment.Continue to breastfeed your baby if they have watery diarrhea, even when traveling to get treatment.
