A doctor from Sierra Leone has been medically evacuated to the United States for treatment of Ebola. He will be treated at a medical facility in Omaha, Nebraska, which has been involved inthe treatment of a number of healthcare workers evacuated from West Africa. In other news, a recently opened laboratory has significantly reduced the time taken for Ebola test results. Results are now available within 48 hours in contrast to the 9 day delay prior to the opening of the new UK-run lab.
