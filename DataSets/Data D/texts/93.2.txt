Sept. 3: Third U.S. missionary doctor with Ebola, Rick Sacra, flown from Liberia to Omaha, Nebraska, for treatment. Officials announce human trials for two vaccines, and U.S. contract to speed up testing experimental ZMapp treatment. Sept. 8: United States to send field hospital to Liberia. Sept. 9: Fourth Ebola patient, who asks not to be unidentified, flown to Atlanta for treatment from Sierra Leone.
