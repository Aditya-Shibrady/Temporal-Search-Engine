This section presents the overall framework for malaria M E, and selected programmatic output, outcome and impact indicators for monitoring programmatic performance and progress of Global Fund-supported malaria programs. The indicators are aligned with recommendations of the WHO Global Malaria Program and Roll Back Malaria M E Reference Group (MERG). Indicator selections are suggested to minimize the reporting burden on programs, focusing on the set of the most common indicators recommended for each specific activity area (including community and health system strengthening in the context of malaria control)
