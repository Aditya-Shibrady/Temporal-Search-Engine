The current outbreak in west Africa, (first cases notified in March 2014), is the largest and most complex Ebola outbreaksince the Ebola virus was first discoveredin 1976. There have been more cases and deaths in this outbreak than all others combined. It has also spread between countries starting in Guinea then spreading across land borders toSierra Leone and Liberia, by air (1 traveller only) to Nigeria, and by land(1 traveller) to Senegal.
