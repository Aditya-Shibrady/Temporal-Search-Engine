The first outbreak of Ebola Virus Disease (EVD) ocurred in The Democratic Republic of Congo, in 1976 having 318 cases and 280 deaths for a case fatality of 88%. Since then 24 more outbreaks has occured in multiples African countries. The current outbreak is affecting six countries (Guinea, Sierra Leone, Liberia, Mali, United States of America and Spain) and three countries (Nigeria, Senegal and Spain) has been declared free of transmission. As of December 21, a total of 19,497 confirmed, probable and suspected cases and 7,588 deaths have been reported, for 39% of case fatality.
