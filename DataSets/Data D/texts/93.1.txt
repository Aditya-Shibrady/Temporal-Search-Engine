Aug. 2: U.S. missionary physician, Kent Brantly, infected with Ebola in Liberia, flown to Atlanta for treatment. Aug. 5: Second U.S. missionary with Ebola, Nancy Writebol, flown from Liberia to Atlanta. Aug. 12: WHO says deaths top 1,000, approves use of unproven drugs or vaccines. Aug. 21: The two missionary workers are released from hospital on Aug. 19 and Aug. 21, free of Ebola.
