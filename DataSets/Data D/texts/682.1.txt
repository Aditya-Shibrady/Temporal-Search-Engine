These are a group of RNA viruses which range from milder disorders (such as the Scandinavian condition nephropathia epidemica) to conditions of high infectivity and lethality which can progress rapidly to high fever, shock and death.Patients present with fever, myalgia, diarrhoea and vomiting, pleuritic pain, shock and bleeding tendency.Health.Specific viruses include:Marburg and Ebola viruses: found in the Sudan, Zaire and Kenya.Lassa fever: found in Nigeria, Sierra Leone and Liberia.
